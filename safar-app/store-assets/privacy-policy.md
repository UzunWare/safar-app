# Privacy Policy for Safar

**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

## Overview

Safar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Safar mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
- Optional profile information (name, preferences)

### Learning Data
To provide our service, we collect:
- Learning progress (words learned, lessons completed, quiz scores)
- Spaced repetition review history
- XP points and streak data
- Notification preferences

### Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- Device information (operating system, app version)
- Usage analytics (features used, session duration)
- Error logs and crash reports (for debugging)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve the Safar learning experience
- Sync your progress across devices
- Send review reminders (if you enable notifications)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our educational content
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a PostgreSQL-based backend)
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text
- We implement authentication safeguards to protect your account

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for marketing purposes

We **may** share data with:
- **Supabase** (our backend infrastructure provider) — for data storage and authentication
- **Expo/EAS** (our development platform) — for app analytics and crash reporting
- Law enforcement if required by law

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data — Request a copy of your personal information
- **Delete** your data — Permanently delete your account and all associated data
- **Export** your data — Download your learning progress in JSON format
- **Opt-out** of notifications — Disable review reminders at any time

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at emre@sekine.app.

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data is retained indefinitely while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Analytics data: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained for improvement purposes

## Children's Privacy

Safar is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Third-Party Services

Safar uses the following third-party services:
- **Supabase** — Database and authentication ([Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Expo** — Mobile app infrastructure ([Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy))

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

## Offline Usage

Safar works offline. When offline:
- Your learning progress is stored locally on your device
- Data syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet
- No data is transmitted while offline

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of Safar after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
