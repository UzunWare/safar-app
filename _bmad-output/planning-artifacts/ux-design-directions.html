Selected Direction

Why "Dual-World" Was Chosen

Direction 3 uniquely serves both the sacred and practical dimensions of Quranic learning. The dark immersive theme for lesson screens honors the gravity of engaging with divine text, creating a focused, distraction-free environment where Arabic words feel luminous and alive against the midnight canvas.

Meanwhile, the light parchment theme for dashboards and progress screens provides the clarity and ease needed for utility tasks: checking progress, reviewing word lists, and navigating between lessons. Users instinctively sense the transition between "study mode" and "overview mode."

The gold accent palette bridges both worlds, maintaining visual continuity and brand identity regardless of which theme is active. This approach also gives the development team clear, context-based rules for when to apply each theme, reducing ambiguity in implementation.
