# Product Brief: safar-project

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Every day, over a billion Muslims stand in prayer reciting words they don't understand. They've memorized surahs phonetically since childhood. They can read Arabic script fluently. But meaning remains locked away - accessible only through translations that create dependency rather than understanding.

The problem isn't lack of resources. It's that existing solutions teach Arabic vocabulary as isolated islands - disconnected words to be memorized one by one. ### Problem Impact

This isn't an inconvenience. It's a daily spiritual disconnect experienced during the most sacred moments of Muslim life:

- **Prayer becomes recitation, not conversation.** Five times daily, Muslims speak directly to Allah in words they don't understand.
- **Quran reading becomes performance, not comprehension.** Millions have memorized entire surahs without grasping their meaning.

### Proposed Solution

Safar reveals the structure of Arabic that was always there but hidden.

**The root-word learning system:** Instead of teaching words in isolation, Safar organizes vocabulary around 3-letter roots. **Gamification that creates habit:** Daily streaks, XP progression, spaced repetition - the engagement mechanics proven by Duolingo, applied to sacred learning. Short sessions (5-10 minutes) that fit into busy lives while building lasting retention.

## Success Metrics

### North Star Metric

**Words Mastered** â€” The total number of Quranic words users can reliably identify and understand.

**Operational definition:** A word is "mastered" when:
1. User has seen the word in learning mode
2. Correctly identified its meaning in 3+ review sessions

**Why this metric:** Words Mastered measures learning outcome â€” the actual transformation we're creating. If this number grows across the user base, we're achieving our mission of helping Muslims understand what they recite.

### Activation Metric

**Definition:** A user is "activated" when they have:
1. Completed 1 full lesson AND
2. Interacted with root connections (tapped a root or related word) AND

**Target:** 50% of signups reach activation within 7 days.

**Why this matters:** Activation captures the "aha moment" â€” the realization that Arabic is a system, not a word list. Users who experience this moment are far more likely to retain and convert.
